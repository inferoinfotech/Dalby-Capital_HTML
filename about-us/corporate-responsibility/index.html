About us

Long-term thinking, transparent action, and ethical leadership

Corporate responsibility isn’t a side initiative — it’s embedded in our DNA. From how we manage investments to how we engage with our people, clients, and the community, we act with integrity, accountability and care.

We believe that financial success and positive impact go hand in hand. Our approach is grounded in the belief that businesses have a broader role to play in supporting sustainability, advancing social wellbeing, and maintaining standards of governance.

ESG Integration 86% of our core portfolio services actively incorporate ESG considerations.
Community Partnerships 78% of teams participated in charity or community initiatives last year.
Sustainable Practices 52% reduction in internal paper and plastic use over the past 3 years.
Governance Reviews 92% of corporate clients strengthened their governance with our support.
Client Impact 71% of clients reported improved ESG alignment in their strategies.

Environmental responsibility

Reducing our carbon footprint for a brighter future

We understand the importance of environmental responsibility — both as a business and as stewards of capital. We’ve taken steps to reduce our operational footprint and partner with suppliers who share our values.

We also support clients seeking to align their investment portfolios with environmental goals, offering tailored ESG and sustainable investment options across asset classes.

Our focus includes:

  • Minimising office waste and energy use
  • Encouraging digital-first documentation
  • Promoting ESG-aligned investment options
  • Low-emission and climate-conscious portfolios
  • Participating in sustainability initiatives
  • Sourcing eco-friendly office supplies

Social impact & community engagement

Supporting communities, empowering individuals, and giving back

We believe strong communities build stronger futures. That’s why we support a range of local programs, charitable causes, and financial education initiatives that aim to create meaningful, lasting impact — not just for today, but for generations to come.

Whether it's through volunteer work, donations, or educational partnerships, we encourage our team and our clients to get involved. Together, we can foster inclusive growth and strengthen local networks.

  • Volunteer leave offered for community involvement
  • Ongoing support for youth financial literacy programs
  • Donations aligned with grassroots initiatives
  • Inclusive hiring policies in place company-wide

Governance & ethical standards

Strong governance
underpins
everything
we do

Trust is the foundation of our business — and we work hard to earn and maintain it. Our governance practices are designed to ensure ethical decision-making, client-first service, and ongoing accountability across all levels of the firm.

We are transparent in our processes, disciplined in our oversight, and committed to operating with the highest ethical standards in both how we serve clients and run our business.

Our governance commitment includes:

  • Independent compliance and risk oversight
  • Regular board and policy reviews
  • Code of conduct and ethics training for all staff
  • Alignment with fiduciary standards
  • Whistleblower protections
  • Ongoing monitoring of regulatory developments
